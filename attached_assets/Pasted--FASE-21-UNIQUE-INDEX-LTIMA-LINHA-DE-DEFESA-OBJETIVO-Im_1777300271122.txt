---

### 2. Ver duplicidade por order_id

sql
SELECT order_id, COUNT() as total
FROM public.nfe_emissoes
GROUP BY order_id
HAVING COUNT() > 1;

---

### 3. Ver duplicidade por numero

sql
SELECT numero, COUNT() as total
FROM public.nfe_emissoes
GROUP BY numero
HAVING COUNT() > 1;

---

### 4. Ver duplicidade combinada

sql
SELECT order_id, numero, COUNT() as total
FROM public.nfe_emissoes
GROUP BY order_id, numero
HAVING COUNT() > 1;

---

### 5. Contagem total

sql
