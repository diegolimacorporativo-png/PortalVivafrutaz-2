2️⃣ ADICIONAR BADGE VISUAL "PAGO"

Dentro do componente que renderiza cada pedido:

{order.isPaid && (
  <span
    className="inline-flex items-center gap-1 px-2 py-1 text-xs font-semibold rounded-md bg-green-100 text-green-800 border border-green-300"

)}
